The SSO is thrilled to have the support of the Frank and Ellen Remai Foundation – this season, donations up to $500,000 to the SSO are matched by the Foundation.

Music plays an integral role in everyone’s lives. Whether the distraction on your commute, the beat that causes spontaneous dancing with friends, the tune that reminds you of that first kiss, the exhilarating backdrop of your favourite movie, or your orchestra in full flight – the musical soundtrack of our lives is what makes life worth living.

Over the last few years, the SSO has been leaning into our mission statement: the Saskatoon Symphony Orchestra shares the joy, hope, and community of making music. Against all odds, we’ve been able to keep your SSO growing and sharing our music with you in the hall and hundreds of thousands of people online. There is much to celebrate!

But now it is time to turn our attention to ensuring a robust future for your SSO.

We’re incredibly proud of what we achieved during the deep challenges of the last two years, including keeping our musicians and staff working through the entire pandemic. We’re excited that we can share our streaming platform with many arts organizations in Saskatchewan.

Your gift to the SSO supports

  • our performances and our performers
  • continuing to grow the SSO and strengthen our community partnerships
  • expanding of our online offerings to increase accessibility to our concerts
  • our educational programming for youth
  • our outreach programs like our online classes and performances for seniors
  • preparing to take the orchestra through to its 100th anniversary season – ambitious plans and growth are needed to make the SSO vitalized for the future

Our goal is to ensure that our musicians, our patrons, and our community have a strong vibrant organization for generations to come.

The SSO has grown strong through some very difficult times in the past, and we can’t wait to see what the future has in score!
